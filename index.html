How to Find Your Laptop UUID

On Windows:

  1. Press Windows Key + R → type cmd → Enter.
  2. Type: wmic csproduct get uuid
  3. Your laptop’s UUID will be displayed.

On macOS:

  1. Apple menu → About This Mac → System Report.
  2. Under Hardware Overview, look for Hardware UUID.
